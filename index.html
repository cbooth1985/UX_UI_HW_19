About Chris

After serving the community for ten years as a Firefighter/Paramedic, Chris decided to pursue his passion for business and real estate.

In just 7 years, Chris has sold over 200 homes with over 50 million dollars in total sales volume. Chris’ desire to deliver excellent customer service and communication is what sets him apart from the rest.

Being instilled with a strong work ethic, gives him the motivation and drive to work hard each day. As with health care and real estate, the internet is always changing and requires you to stay informed. Through his motivation and love for research, Chris will always be up to date on new idea’s allowing him to keep being creative and innovative.
